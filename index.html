Welcome to blur

Almost thirty years on from the Mile End concert, Blur are going to play Wembley Stadium for the first time. Tickets go on sale on Friday 18 November at 10am. The gig will take place on Saturday 8 July 2023. It will bring band members Damon Albarn, Graham Coxon, Alex James and Dave Rowntree back together to perform some of their biggest hits. It will be their only UK appearance and first headline show since 2015. Coxon said: "I'm really looking forward to playing with my Blur brothers again and revisiting all those great songs… Blur live shows are always amazing for me. A nice guitar and an amp turned right up and loads of smiling faces."
